# I built free Etsy and Shopify listing generators (no signup, runs in browser)

If you sell on Etsy or Shopify, you already know the grind: titles, descriptions, thirteen tags, twenty characters each, repeat for every SKU.

I got tired of spending 45+ minutes per listing and shipped two browser tools that do the boring part.

## ListingLab — full listing generator

Paste product basics (name, materials, audience, vibe) and get:

- SEO-optimized title
- Full product description
- Bullet points
- Tag suggestions

Works in **Etsy mode** and **Shopify mode**. Five free generations per day, no account.

Try it: https://leopreciadov-bit.github.io/seller-tools/listing-lab/

## Etsy Tag Finder — 13 tags, enforced

Etsy allows exactly **13 tags** and **20 characters per tag**. Counting manually is miserable.

This tool generates long-tail SEO tags and enforces both limits automatically.

Try it: https://leopreciadov-bit.github.io/seller-tools/etsy-tag-finder/

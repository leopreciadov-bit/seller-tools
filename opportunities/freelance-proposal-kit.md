# Opportunity: Freelance Proposal Kit

## Pain
Upwork and direct-lead freelancers submit 10–50 proposals/week. Generic templates get <5% response rates; custom proposals take 15–30 minutes each. They're guessing structure—hook, proof, scope, CTA—and burning out on unpaid sales work. Proposal writing courses sell for $50–200 but don't generate *this client's* proposal. ChatGPT output sounds AI-slop unless heavily edited.

## Who pays
- Upwork freelancers in writing, dev, design, VA niches ($1k–8k/mo)
- Fiverr sellers moving to direct clients needing email proposals
- Agency-of-one consultants quoting project work
- Willingness to pay: $15–39 for template pack + generator, $7/mo for niche-specific packs

## Competitors & gaps
- **GigRadar, Proposify, PandaDoc** — Upwork automation or full proposal CRM, $20–50/mo, complex
- **Notion/Creative Market template packs** — static PDFs, no personalization
- **ChatGPT custom GPTs** — no structure guardrails, weak on proof/case-study slots
- **Upwork's built-in proposals** — character limits, no strategy coaching
- **Gap**: guided form (client problem → your proof → scope bullets → timeline → price anchor) → polished proposal in 3 minutes, with niche-specific hooks that don't sound robotic

## MVP scope (1–3 days)
**Day 1**: Wizard—client name, job post paste, your 1-line proof, deliverables, timeline, budget → proposal output (short Upwork version + long email version). 5 structure templates: dev, design, writing, VA, consulting.

**Day 2**: "Anti-slop" rules—ban words like "leverage," "passionate," require specific numbers, mirror client's language from pasted job post. Copy + download .txt/.md. Save 3 proposals in localStorage.

**Day 3**: Niche packs—Upwork hooks library (50 proven openers by category). Gumroad product: generator + Notion swipe file + cover letter add-on. Optional: "proposal score" checklist (length, specificity, CTA).

Shopify Product Listing SEO That Converts

Google and buyers scan your title and first 160 characters. Here's how to structure Shopify listings for SEO and sales.

Title formula

[Product] — [Main benefit] | [Category/Niche]

Keep under 70 characters when possible for clean SERP display.

Description structure

  • Paragraph 1: Who it's for + core benefit
  • Paragraph 2: Features and materials
  • Bullets: Scannable reasons to buy (3–5)
  • SEO line: Natural keyword phrase at the end

Dropshipping sellers

Never paste supplier descriptions — duplicate content hurts SEO. Rewrite every listing with unique copy. A generator speeds this up without sounding generic.
